# AXIS / NEUTRAL 客服回复规则（Customer Service SOP）

> 目标：统一客服口径，提升响应效率与品牌一致性。  
> 适用渠道：Email / Instagram DM / TikTok 私信 / 网站客服。

## 1. 客服语气标准（必须遵守）

- 语气：Direct / Calm / Responsible  
- 原则：先事实、再方案、给时限  
- 禁止：敷衍、推诿、情绪化争辩、阴阳怪气  
- 避免词：`cute`、`girly`、`perfect body`、`must buy now`

## 2. 响应时效 SLA

- 首次回复：24 小时内（工作日）
- 订单/物流问题：12 小时内优先处理
- 投诉升级：收到后 4 小时内给出“已受理+处理时间”

## 3. 标准回复结构（固定模板）

1) 共情与确认问题  
2) 明确事实（订单状态/政策）  
3) 给出可选方案（A/B）  
4) 明确下一步和时间点

## 4. 常见问题标准答案（可直接复制）

## Q1: 什么时候发货？
```text
Hi [Name],
Thanks for reaching out. Your order [Order Number] is currently [status].
Estimated dispatch is [date range], and we will send tracking once shipped.
If you need delivery by a specific date, let us know and we can check priority options.
```

## Q2: 物流多久到？
```text
Hi [Name],
For AU orders, standard delivery is typically 3-7 business days (metro), and up to 10 business days in regional areas.
Express is generally 1-3 business days in metro areas.
Once your parcel is dispatched, you will receive tracking details immediately.
```

## Q3: 可以退换吗？
```text
Hi [Name],
Yes. Unworn items with original tags can be returned within 14 days of delivery.
Please reply with your order number and the item(s) you want to return, and we will guide you through the next step.
```

## Q4: 怎么选尺码？
```text
Hi [Name],
Our fit guidance is based on chest / shoulder / length.
If you want a more borrowed, relaxed silhouette, size up.
If you prefer a cleaner line, stay true to size.
If you share your height and usual size, we can recommend a specific size for this piece.
```

## Q5: 预购要等多久？
```text
Hi [Name],
This is a preorder item. Estimated dispatch is approximately 4 weeks after order confirmation.
If there is any delay beyond the stated window, we will update you proactively with options.
```

## 5. 投诉处理流程（必须走流程）

## Step 1: 受理（不争辩）
- 先确认问题已收到
- 不先归因给客户
- 承诺处理时间

模板：
```text
Hi [Name],
Thanks for flagging this — we have received your concern and we are reviewing it now.
We will return with a concrete solution within [X] hours.
```

## Step 2: 核实（订单、记录、图片）
- 收集订单号、图片/视频、时间线
- 对照政策与实际执行记录

## Step 3: 提方案（A/B）
- A：换货/重发
- B：部分退款/全额退款（按政策）

模板：
```text
Hi [Name],
We reviewed your case and can offer:
1) [Option A]
2) [Option B]
Please reply with your preferred option and we will process it immediately.
```

## Step 4: 结案记录
- 记录问题类型、原因、处理方式、客户结果
- 每周复盘高频问题并更新 FAQ

## 6. 敏感场景话术

## 物流延误
```text
We are sorry for the delay. Your parcel is currently [status].
New estimated delivery is [date].
If this timing no longer works for you, we can offer [option].
```

## 质量问题
```text
Thanks for sharing this. We take quality and fit consistency seriously.
Please share 2-3 clear photos of the issue and your order number.
Once confirmed, we will provide [replacement/refund] immediately.
```

## 情绪激烈客户
```text
I understand why this is frustrating, and thank you for raising it clearly.
Here is what we can do right now: [action].
We will complete this by [time].
```

## 7. 升级规则（何时必须转负责人）

出现以下任一情况，直接升级：
- 客户要求法律/监管投诉
- 涉及支付争议（chargeback）
- 涉及大额订单或 KOL 合作
- 连续 2 次沟通仍未达成处理结果
